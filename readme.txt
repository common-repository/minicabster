== Description ==
With Minicabster plugin, you can earn money by recommending us to your customers through your website.
Any bookings received from your premises will be logged and you will receive up to 5% of the amount paid for each journey.
It's a really simple way to increase your revenue while also providing your customers with great service.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `minicabster.zip` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Unzip `minicabster.zip`
3. Activate the minicabster plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
4. In 'Appearance' menu, choose 'Widgets' submenu in WordPress
5. Look for 'Minicabster' and drag or add to the desired Area
6. Enter the minicabster affiliate token. In case you do not have the token, register at the provided URL.
7. Enter the widget width and height. This is optional.
8. Enter the Cab ID. Entering the cab id would mean that the quotes to be received are all for a specific cab operator. This is optional.
9. Save the widget
